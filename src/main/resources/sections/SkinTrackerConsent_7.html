What side effects or risks can I expect from being in the study?

This study does not involve receiving a study drug or any medical intervention. Any side effects of medications that you start while in the study or are taking before starting the study are unlikely to be related to using SkinTracker. If you experience side effects during the study, you will need to follow-up with your routine medical provider. A non-medical risk of participating in this study includes breach of personal information that is stored in SkinTracker. The app development company RedBlink Inc. has several safety practices in place to ensure that data that you input into the app is secure. However, as with all personal information, there is a potential chance for loss of privacy due to a security breach. RedBlink Inc. will not have access to your medical records and will only have access to information that you store in SkinTracker.

  • Unknown Risks: The experimental mobile app may have risks that no one knows about yet. The researchers will let you know if they learn anything that might make you change your mind about participating in the study.
  • For more information about risks and side effects, ask your study doctor.
