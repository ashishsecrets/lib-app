How long will my child be in the study?

Your child will be asked to use SkinTracker for 12 months. You and your child will input the requested information at least once each week. Additionally, you and your child will visit with the research team 5 times.

Can my child stop being in the study?

Yes. You can decide to stop at any time. Tell the study doctor if you and/or your child are thinking about stopping or decide to stop. He or she will tell you how to stop your participation safely.
