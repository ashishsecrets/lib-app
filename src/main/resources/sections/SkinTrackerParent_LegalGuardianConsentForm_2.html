Possible Risks

There are risks to taking part in a research study. Some potential risks of participation in this study include:

  • Loss of privacy in the event of a data breach of information stored by the app.
  • Increased awareness of skin activity causing stress or concerns.

Of note, the privacy of your child’s data is protected to the highest extent possible using data encryption using strong SSL certificates and compliance with HIPAA.

Possible Benefits

Some potential benefits of your child’s participation in this study include:

  • Having better surveillance of your child’s skin condition.
  • Helping to improve research efforts in the future.
  • More frequent monitoring of your child’s skin condition by a physician.

Your Other Options

You do not have to participate in this study. Your other choices may include:

  • Getting treatment or care for your child’s condition without being in a study.
  • Taking part in another study.
  • Getting no treatment or receiving symptomatic care to relieve your child’s symptoms and discomfort.
